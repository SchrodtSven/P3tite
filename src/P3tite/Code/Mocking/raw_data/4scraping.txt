Dr. Henry "Hank" Pym, Ph.D,[19] was an American entomologist, biochemist, roboticist, engineer and physicist and "Scientific Adventurer".
[13] He was considered one of the smartest humans on Earth. He discovered the Pym Particles, subatomic particles which can increase or decrease the size and mass of objects or living beings.
[2] He built a suit and for a time, became the original Ant-Man, fighting beside his future wife, Janet Van Dyne, who had taken on the mantle of the Wasp.[2]
[7] Alongside Van Dyne, he became a founding member of the Avengers, "Earth's Mightiest Heroes".[20] Pym was the creator of the deadly android, Ultron.
[21] He was also able to modify his Pym Particles to allow him to grow in size to become Giant-Man,[22] and later Goliath.[4] After a nervous breakdown he changed identities to Yellowjacket,
[6] and then later to the Wasp to honor his deceased ex-wife.[5] His skills in science also led him to be appointed Earth's Scientist Supreme.[3]
